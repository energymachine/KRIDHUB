      About KRID

      KRID is a creative platform merging sound, fashion and frequency. Born from underground hi-tech psytrance, KRID curates intense audio experiences and crafts UV-reactive, geometry-driven psywear engineered for the dancefloor and beyond. We are a collective: artists, designers and seekers exploring sound alchemy and inner evolution.

      • Mission: Amplify consciousness through bass, light and wearable art.
      • Vision: A global collective where music becomes light and clothes become portals.
